About Us

Founded in 2010, SuZhou Runest Packaging is a one-stop packaging solution supplier. Our main products include gift boxes, paper cans/tubes, paper bags, brochures, catalogs, display boxes, paper inserts, labels, and various customized paper packaging products.

SuZhou Runest Packaging specializes in custom paper packaging solutions. We provide one-stop OEM & ODM services for global brands.
